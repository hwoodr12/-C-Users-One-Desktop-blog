Histograms

Posted on 2017.10.05 · Tagged in histograms, visualization

Histograms are diagrams consisting of rectangles whose area is proportional to the frequency of a variable and whose width is equal to the class interval. library(Lahman) library(ggplot2) library(sqldf) query<-"SELECT weight FROM Master" result<-sqldf(query) ggplot()+ geom_histogram(data = result,aes(x=weight),color="blue",fill="white",bins=50)+ ggtitle("Body Weight Distribution for Baseball Players")
